The mission of the Indian Health Center has continued to provide world-class care to the Native American/Alaskan Native community for 30 years now because of the support from people like yourself. Your donations allow our organization to help thousands of people a year and we thank you for that.

If you've never donated before, take a moment to look below at the many ways you can help. Donate what you can afford as every bit helps.

Because the Indian Health Center of Santa Clara Valley is a nonprofit organization, we rely on support from individuals, foundations and corporations to enable us to offer opportunities to provide health services for American Indians, Alaskan Natives and other uninsured or underinsured members of our community.

Your gift will support the core, ongoing programs and services of the Indian Health Center of Santa Clara Valley including:

control_point

Medical Department

Medical Department offers general medicine (acute and chronic), prenatal and pediatric care, women's health, birth control, pregnancy testing, physicals for work and school, low cost immunizations, public health nursing and treatment for sexually transmitted infections.

Dental Department

Dental Department offers all phases of general dentistry for children and adults, including, emergency care, exams, x-rays, cleaning, gum treatment, fillings, sealants, crowns, extractions, root canals therapy, cancer screenings, full and partial dentures, orthodontic screenings and referrals, bite splits and dental health education.

Counseling Department

Counseling Department offers case management services, mental health counseling, alcohol and other substance abuse counseling, recovery diversion groups, crisis intervention, assessments and evaluations, medication management, and linkages to residential programs.

Community Wellness

Community Wellness & Outreach offers no cost diabetes health education, health screenings, home visits, case management, injury prevention education, prenatal education,, HIV counseling and testing, native youth group, fitness program, transportation, and community health and wellness activities.

Women, Infants and Children Nutrition Department

Women, Infants and Children Nutrition Department (WIC) offers nutrition education and food vouchers for low-income women and children, providing healthy foods at no cost, nutrition counseling and breast feeding support. Medical nutrition counseling is also provided for controlling diabetes, blood pressure, cholesterol, and weight.
