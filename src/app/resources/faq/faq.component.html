The Indian Health Center (IHC) is a Non-Profit Community Health Clinic, Patient Centered Health Home Medical Clinic, and Wellness Center for all who live in Santa Clara County. We provide Medical Care, Dental Care, WIC Services, Counseling and Mental Health Service, and additionally we support our Santa Clara County Community through our Community Wellness and Outreach Department. Please visit our departments' pages to learn more about the services they provide, and visit our locations to learn more!

No. You do not need health insurance to be seen at the Indian Health Center. However we do take Medi-Cal and some other insurances.

The Indian Health Center does provides Low-Cost, Sliding Scale Healthcare services based on your income level. The IHC can also help you find health coverage through Covered California. And, if you are an American Indian you may be eligible to enroll in our Ross Gritts program to help with the costs of medical or dental care.
There are currently no American Indian Physicians at the Indian health center. However, we do have American Indian counselors and staff to help you navigate our care and health home.
The Indian Health Center does provide some holistic healing services. Please visit our CWO and Counseling Departments to learn more about what non-western medicinal services we provide.
The Indian Health Center does provide some traditional healing services. Please contact our counseling department for more information.
The IHC Provides a wide array of health care services. As a Patient Centered Health Home, we are our patients' "home" for all their medical needs. From our Medical Site, we provide great primary medical care with the help of our physicians and their assistants, who can refer our patients to our Dental Department for general dental needs, to outside specialists if needed, to WIC for family care, nutrition and development, and to our Wellness Center for Counseling, youth programming, elder care, and Diabetes prevention and management. We are an integrated community health care clinic here to serve you!

The Indian Health Center has a long-standing tradition of providing great service, in a welcoming environment. When you come to the IHC, you are embraced with the kindness and generosity that is traditional to our American Indian Heritage. With all the services we provide, and being so well connected to the Silicon Valley Community, we can help you thrive in health and wellness, in one circle of trust, friendship, and wellness.
