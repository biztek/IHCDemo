If patients are not eligible for any of the above programs, they will be placed on the medical self-pay Sliding Fee Scale or asked to pay full-fee. Depending on the level of income and their household size, patients will be asked to pay 40%, 50%, 75% or 100% of the total visit charges. If clients do not have money on them, they will be billed for the visit. Bills are sent out once a month.

Self-pay (both Sliding Fee Scale and full-fee) patients are expected to pay 100% of the lab charges on the day of their visit. The lab offers reduced rates to the clients of the Indian Health Center.

Dental

We accept Medi-Cal and most private insurance plans. Uninsured persons with a certain level of income may qualify for our "Discount Program" and receive services below our already reduced fees (proof of income is required to apply).
